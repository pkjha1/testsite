We turn investments into profits via architecture.

We have been a general designer for 12 years. We successfully create and develop large complex projects at all phases - from conception to putting them into operation.

Concept

Consulting is the shortest way to achieve the customer's goals. We offer individual solutions based on our practical experience and constantly developing knowledge. Site Analysis
We thoroughly explore the site, its surroundings and its location, analyzing the impact on its environment, physical and regulatory constraints. We identify weaknesses and emphasize the strengths of the site.
Marketing
We develop practical solutions that meet the goals of our clients, develop the urban environment and increase the value of the project.
Audit of previously developed concepts
We give an expert evaluation of ready-made concepts. We will point out the pros and cons, uncover the untapped potential and opportunities. We offer alternatives to improve the quality and value of the project.
Forecasting technical and economic indicators
To forecast the economic potential of the site, we determine its strengths and weaknesses, take into account the opportunities and threats for the developer. Based on the received data, we simulate the technical and economic potential of the site.
Project Reconception
We reveal the best solution for site development. We will find the only correct practical and cost-effective solution that will improve the urban environment, bring comfort to residents and the client profits.
Audit of projects at all stages
We are ready to connect to the project at any stage.
Concept Development
After the study of the data on the site and the functional purpose of the object, we accumulate ideas and begin to develop the concept. Based on the position of the object classes, scale, functional type draw up cost-benefit analysis of the investment project and find the best solution to meet customer's requirements and needs of the city.

Architecture

Ideal project maintains a balance between the needs of the end user, the profitability of the project and the requirements of the urban environment. We create profitable projects that are valuable for the clients, the city and the residents.

Master Plan Analysis
At the stage of Master plan analysis, we thoroughly study the site, its surroundings, its role in the formation of the urban environment. We define the constraints that will affect the shape and location of the future structure. At this stage, you can determine the approximate technical and economic indicators, the maximum permissible building density, calculate the urban planning parameters for the site such as the necessary number of parking lots, recreation areas We have developed a tool, due to which we obtain the data on insolation at the site, the illumination of neighboring territories and their impact on the projected objects.
Schematic Design
At this stage we evaluate urban, architectural, structural and MEP conditions of the future project and create distinct aesthetic image and main concept. Concept design allows to get rough estimates of object’s appearance, room layouts and construction costs. Sound concept is the key to success of the whole project.
Design Development Phase
At this stage we use results of schematic design and, if necessary, revise them to create detailed BIM model, which allows us to efficiently and accurately estimate building’s parameters after the decisions about structures and materials are finalized. Design documents are then ensured to satisfy building and legal requirements as well as our high standards to aesthetics and project’s concept. By the end of this phase we are ready to present design documents sufficient for permit application and final estimation of construction budget and schedule.
Architectural visualization & animation
3D visualization allows us to observe the project at any given time of the day and in different weather conditions in its real surroundings. It is a powerful tool for both architect and client as it helps us showcase room layouts in fine details as well as the concept of whole project. For even stronger sense of presence we can provide animation.
Construction supervision
Construction supervision is conducted during the construction phase. We control that construction works are carried out according to blueprints, specifications and construction documents. The aim of construction supervision is to prevent discrepancies between documentation and construction. It guarantees that building is successfully commissioned and meets the original investment intent.
3D - 6D проектирование
Our design starts with a 3D BIM model. It enhances cooperation between architects and engineers and allows for more complex holistic engineering analyses. In cooperation with project management and contractors schedule (4D) and construction costs (5D) are incorporated into the model. Finally, facility management and maintenance (6D) are also supported by information model giving access to energy, heat consumption and other maintenance parameters.
Construction Documents
We provide detailed blueprints for construction while keeping the project true to its original concept. Thanks to contemporary approach to design we can promptly implement unforeseen changes in line with client’s needs. Large collective experience and company’s accumulated resources increase speed and quality of documents delivery. Interactive models allow to predict and solve possible construction problems several steps ahead.
Promotional and sales materials
In order to better evaluate of the functional use of the apartment, compare the functionality of different apartments, and finally consciously choose the most suitable apartment layout for buyer`s family, we developed an innovative method for assessing the functionality of apartments. For each residential complex with PRO-planning we have developed an unique offer - a passport for the functional use of the apartment. With such visual materials, the choice of apartment will become obvious to every buyer.

We offer innovative engineering solutions tailored to client’s needs as efficient engineering always implies result-oriented strategic approach. We employ scientific knowledge and extensive experience while considering every aspect of facility construction and operation to provide rational and reliable design solutions.

Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning (HVAC)
We design wide range of HVAC systems to satisfy client’s requirements to the project. In order to ensure comfortable thermal conditions we use CFD modeling for heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems.
Plumbing
We provide full range of plumbing design, such as tap water and firefighting water supply systems, sanitary and combined sewers, gravitational and siphonic rainwater drainage systems, lawn sprinkler systems. Starting with water consumption analysis of whole facility and ending with connection of end user to the system we cover all scales of plumbing design services.
Electrical engineering
Our expertise in electrical engineering encompasses power supply systems, lighting surge protection, lighting (including designer lighting), electrical equipment, power distribution, busbar systems, electrical load calculations for all scales of projects.
Energy passport
Develop energy passport of the building according to new regulations
Automated building management systems
We design deeply integrated building management systems of various functionality and complexity for both standard and premium residential, commercial and office buildings.

Structural design

Our structural engineering team collaborates with architects and MEP engineers while working on shared building information model. We avoid conservative solutions, instead we adopt structural system and materials efficiently with regard to structural integrity, reliability and cost effectiveness. By employing BIM technologies we reach high level of automation and reduce amount of rework and errors. It allows us to deliver high quality documents more accurately in less time.

Schematic Design
The most efficient structural system is chosen based on architectural sketches and material expenditure can be roughly estimated.
Design Development Phase
In collaboration with architects structural engineers pass through series of iterations to reach optimal design and finalize structural solutions. Concluded design is then ready for construction permit application. Based on adopted structural system material cost estimates are revised.

Sketch of style and mood

3D visualization and visualization for VR

Specification of components and materials

Interior concept

Design project

Detailed Design Documentation

Designer supervision
