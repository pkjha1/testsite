BIM is a design by creating a unified digital model of a building with a high degree of detail for each phase of the project. The method allows to realize the future building with all the architectural and engineering solutions in virtual reality even on the schematic design phase. The entire process of erecting a building is also possible to model both in time and in value.

The method allows to realize the future building with all the architectural and engineering solutions in virtual reality even on the schematic design phase. The entire process of erecting a building is also possible to model both in time and in value.

BIM allows

  • To improve the quality of the project and the development of project documentation

  • To significantly reduce the cost of construction and the timing of the project

  • To see all the details already in the design process, necessary for making key decisions

  • To optimize costs and resources and increase the efficiency of the project work

  • To establish real-time communication between designers and builders

  • To decrease the quantity of changes and reduce errors during the construction phase
